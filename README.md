# Project_EMP

## Employee

* EmpID
* Name
* Payment
    * Hourly : hourly rate
    * Flat : fixed salary
    * Commission : commission rate, fixed salary
* Payment method
    * Mailed to postal address
    * Pickup from paymaster
    * Deposit into bank account

---

## Hourly employee

* Hourly rate
* Max 8 hours
* Paid 1.5 more for extra hours
* Paid avery Friday

## Flat salary

* Fixed salary
* Paid last working day of the month

## Commission

* Flat salary
* Commission rate
* Submit sales receipts
    * Date
    * Amount of sale
* Paid every Friday
